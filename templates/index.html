🤖 How It Works

This AI model uses a 2-layer LSTM (Long Short-Term Memory) neural network trained on 10+ years of historical gold price data. It analyzes 60-day price patterns to predict future trends with high accuracy.


📊 Model Performance

• Architecture: LSTM (100 units) + Dropout + Dense layers
• Training Data: 2015-2024 gold futures (GC=F)
• Input Sequence: 60 days of historical prices
• Prediction Horizon: 1-90 days forward
